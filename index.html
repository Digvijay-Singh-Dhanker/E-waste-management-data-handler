An individual should be very specific in his choices when buying any electric or electronic equipment. Thus a person should reduce his wants. Next, if a technology gets old or outdated, still it could be useful for some with limited utility.
Such equipments with obsolete technology should be donated to other people who could make use of it effectively in their work. This is the concept of reuse.
Last but not the least, if equipment has to be discarded due to non-functioning, it should be handed over only to authorized recycling vendors for recycling, in order to avoid improper dumping.
